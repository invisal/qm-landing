Database Management Made Simple.

A simple, lightweight, and free database management tool, yet powerful and packed with amazing features.

Features.

Cross-Platform

QueryMaster is built with Electron, which means it runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Beginner Friendly

QueryMaster is designed to be easy to use, even for beginners. It's simple and intuitive.

Powerful Auto-Completion

We focused on making the best auto-completion. It's fast, accurate, and context-aware.

Private and Secure

Your data is stored locally and securely. We don't collect any data from you.

Export and Import

You can export and import your databases in SQL format, compatible with MySQL and PostgreSQL.

Cloud Based

You can connect to your cloud databases, such as Amazon RDS, Google Cloud SQL, and more.
